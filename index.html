The History Of Our Hotel

A Journey of Timeless Hospitality: Unraveling the Legacy of LAviva. Founded in 1999, LAviva was born from a vision to create an oasis of luxury and comfort in the heart of Abuja. Over the years, LAviva has blossomed into an icon of hospitality, earning accolades for its impeccable service and attention to detail. Keeping pace with the changing times, LAviva has undergone thoughtful renovations, blending contemporary design with its timeless charm. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped in an ambiance of indulgence, with personalized services and luxurious comforts at every turn. We invite you to be a part of our legacy and experience the enchantment of LAviva. Book your stay today and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime."
